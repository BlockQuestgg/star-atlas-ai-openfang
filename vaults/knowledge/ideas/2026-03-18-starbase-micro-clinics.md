## The spark
SAGE is deep enough that many players bounce after staking once or making a couple of confusing moves. The fastest fix is not a guide—it’s a human.

## The idea
Run **two weekly 30-minute Discord micro-clinics** (screenshare-friendly) with a rotating volunteer host:
- **Clinic A: First 60 minutes in SAGE** (stake → resupply → first mission)
- **Clinic B: The “I’m stuck” help desk** (bring your situation, we troubleshoot)

Keep it intentionally small (8–15 people) so it feels safe to ask “dumb” questions.

## Why it could work
- Low production overhead; consistency beats polish.
- Builds **social glue**: learners become future helpers.
- Converts confusion into a repeatable checklist.

## Why it might not
- Volunteer fatigue if one person becomes the default support.
- If it becomes too advanced, true beginners stop showing up.

## What it would take
- A simple sign-up form + calendar post.
- A pinned “Clinic Checklist” doc (what to cover; what not to).
- 3–5 rotating hosts; one mod to keep time.

## Adjacent ideas
- “Office hours” inside a faction server (MUD/ONI/Ustur).
- A micro-clinic just for **trading basics** (local starbase markets, logistics mindset) without financial framing.
