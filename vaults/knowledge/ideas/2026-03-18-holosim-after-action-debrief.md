## The spark
Holosim chapters are naturally episodic. Communities that treat episodes like “TV night” keep people returning.

## The idea
Host a **weekly 60-minute ‘Chapter Night’**:
1. 10 min: quick recap of last week’s story beats
2. 30 min: group play / stream one participant’s run (or highlight reels)
3. 20 min: **After-Action Debrief** where each faction writes a short in-character dispatch:
   - “What we saw”
   - “What we believe is happening”
   - “What we do next”

Publish the three dispatches in a single Discord/Forum post (MUD/ONI/Ustur). It becomes serialized community lore.

## Why it could work
- Low-lift, repeatable programming.
- Turns gameplay into shareable narrative artifacts.
- Gives faction identity a weekly touchpoint.

## Why it might not
- Needs a consistent host voice to keep it moving.
- If spoilers matter, you’ll need spoiler channels.

## What it would take
- A recurring calendar slot.
- One streamer/screensharer + one “scribe” for dispatches.
- A simple template for the three faction posts.

## Adjacent ideas
- Invite a community artist to illustrate one scene per week.
- “Dispatch swap”: each faction writes what they *think* the other factions are doing.
