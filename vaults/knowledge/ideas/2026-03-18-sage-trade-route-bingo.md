## The spark
Players love having *a reason to log in* that isn’t a massive competition. Bingo is structured, social, and meme-able.

## The idea
Create a **weekly Bingo card** with 16–25 squares of small, non-technical SAGE tasks, e.g.:
- Complete 1 scan mission
- Move to a new sector (or new starbase)
- Craft a basic supply item
- Post one screenshot of your fleet screen
- Help one player in #help (counts as a square)

People post their completed cards/screenshots in a single Discord thread. Optional: “community badge” role for anyone who completes a line.

## Why it could work
- Doesn’t require prizes to feel rewarding.
- Encourages breadth of play (mining/crafting/scanning/social).
- Great content fuel: weekly recap + funniest squares.

## Why it might not
- If squares are too easy, it feels pointless.
- If squares are too hard, it feels like homework.

## What it would take
- One volunteer “Bingo captain” making the template.
- A shared Google Sheet/Canva template.
- A consistent weekly posting time.

## Adjacent ideas
- “Faction Bingo” where a few squares are faction-flavored lore prompts.
- A “new player” bingo with only day-1 achievable squares.
