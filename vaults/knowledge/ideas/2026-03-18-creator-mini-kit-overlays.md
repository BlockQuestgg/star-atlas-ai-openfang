## The spark
The easiest way to increase community content volume is to reduce the ‘blank canvas’ problem.

## The idea
Make a **tiny community creator pack** (Canva + transparent PNGs):
- 3 lower-thirds (“SAGE Tip”, “Trade Route”, “Fleet Build”)
- 3 faction frames (MUD/ONI/Ustur)
- 2 ‘Before/After’ stat comparison layouts
- 2 meme caption templates that fit common screenshots

Then run a **monthly ‘clip jam’**: “Post one clip using any overlay; we feature 10.”

## Why it could work
- Doesn’t require anyone to be a pro editor.
- Makes Star Atlas clips visually consistent (brand-by-community).
- Encourages micro-content, which feeds onboarding.

## Why it might not
- If the pack looks too ‘official’, people may hesitate to remix.
- Needs light coordination to avoid messy branding arguments.

## What it would take
- One design volunteer for 2–3 hours.
- A shared folder + short usage note (“remix encouraged”).
- A weekly feature post to reinforce the habit.

## Adjacent ideas
- A ‘caption bank’ of 30 hook lines (“I thought SAGE was boring until…”) creators can reuse.
- A community “clip of the week” playlist.
