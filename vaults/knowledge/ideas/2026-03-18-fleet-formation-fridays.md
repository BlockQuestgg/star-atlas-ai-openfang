## The spark
Most people learn fleet composition by lurking. A structured weekly thread turns lurking into sharing.

## The idea
Every Friday, post a single prompt:
> **Fleet Formation Friday:** Drop your fleet + your next 7-day goal. Ask one question you want feedback on.

Format template (copy/paste):
- Ships staked (classes/specs):
- Current location/starbase:
- Current loop (mining/crafting/scanning/trade):
- Bottleneck:
- One question:

Community replies with suggestions, memes, and “try this next.”

## Why it could work
- Creates a weekly habit without organizing a full event.
- Generates an archive of real builds and problem-solving.
- Makes experienced players visible (and therefore approachable).

## Why it might not
- Can devolve into flex posts if not moderated.
- New players may feel intimidated without a “beginner-friendly” framing.

## What it would take
- One mod or volunteer to post the prompt weekly.
- A “beginner tag” reaction to encourage supportive replies.

## Adjacent ideas
- “Fleet Roast (friendly)” edition once per month.
- A companion voice room for 45 minutes right after the thread goes live.
