# The Galia Expanse — Setting & History

The Galia Expanse is the vast galactic region where Star Atlas takes place. It is a dangerous, resource-rich frontier populated by three major civilizations: [[factions|MUD, ONI, and Ustur]]. The Galia Expanse serves as both the game map and the narrative setting.

## Geography

The Galia Expanse is divided into sectors, each containing Starbases, asteroid fields, planetary bodies, and anomalies. Key geographic concepts:

- **Safe Zone** — The COP-controlled core region where the Treaty of Peace is enforced. Relative safety for trade and travel.
- **Medium-Risk Zone** — Outer regions with reduced COP presence. PvP encounters possible, higher-value resources.
- **High-Risk Zone** — The frontier. Lawless space with the most valuable resources but greatest danger. Full PvP.
- **Starbases** — Infrastructure hubs scattered across sectors. Each functions as a local economy (see [[sage-overview]]).

## The Known Story

### Pre-History

The Galia Expanse's deep history involves the emergence of three distinct forms of intelligence: human (MUD), alien (ONI), and synthetic (Ustur). Each developed independently before encountering the others in the vast reaches of the galaxy.

### The Convergence War

The defining event in Galia's recent history. When the three civilizations first encountered each other, conflict erupted. The Convergence War was devastating — particularly for humanity (MUD), which suffered dramatic losses.

The war ended with the signing of the **Treaty of Peace**, establishing the Council of Peace (COP) and the three-faction governance structure that persists today.

### 100 Years of Peace

The game's current era marks approximately 100 years since the Treaty of Peace. The anniversary is a significant cultural event across all three factions. During this century of peace, the factions developed trade relationships, shared technologies (to varying degrees), and expanded into new sectors of the Galia Expanse.

However, peace is fragile. Territorial disputes, resource competition, and fundamental philosophical differences between organic and synthetic life continue to create tension.

## The Galia Expanse in Gameplay

The Galia Expanse is where all [[sage-overview|SAGE Labs]] gameplay takes place:

- Fleets navigate between sectors and Starbases
- Resources are mined from asteroid fields and planetary bodies
- Local marketplaces at each Starbase create a distributed economy
- Faction territories influence scanning bonuses and political control
- The risk-zone system creates a gradient from safe trade to high-stakes PvP
