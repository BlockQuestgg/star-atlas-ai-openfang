# SAGE Labs — Gameplay Overview

SAGE (Star Atlas Golden Era) is the primary on-chain gameplay experience in the Star Atlas metaverse. It runs fully on the Solana blockchain, meaning every action — mining, crafting, trading, scanning — is an on-chain transaction with real economic weight.

## What Is SAGE Labs

SAGE Labs is the browser-based economic simulation layer of Star Atlas. Players manage fleets of ships across the [[galia-expanse|Galia Expanse]], engaging in resource extraction, crafting, trade, and exploration. It is accessible at [play.staratlas.com](https://play.staratlas.com).

SAGE Labs is distinct from the Unreal Engine 5 (UE5) visual experience and the Holosim combat module — it is the economic backbone where the player-driven economy lives.

## Core Gameplay Loops

SAGE Labs revolves around three primary activities:

**Mining** — Fleets are sent to asteroid fields and planetary bodies to extract raw resources: Hydrogen, Organics, Iron, and Copper. Mining operations fuel the crafting economy and generate income. Ships require Ammunition to mine.

**Crafting** — Raw materials are refined at Starbases into usable supplies. The four base refined resources (R4) are Fuel, Food, Toolkits, and Ammunition. Advanced crafting produces ship components, upgrades, and trade goods. See [[resources-and-crafting]] for full details.

**Scanning** — Fleets scan sectors for Survey Data Units (SDUs), which are valuable data packages containing resources and information. SDU scanning represents a unique exploration-oriented gameplay loop with faction-based bonus rewards in specific sectors.

## Fleet Management

To participate in SAGE, players must stake their ships into the SAGE Fleet Formation program. Once staked, ships are committed to SAGE and unavailable for use elsewhere until withdrawn.

Fleets consume resources while operating: Fuel for movement, Food for crew sustenance, Ammunition for mining and combat, and Toolkits for ship maintenance. Managing these supply chains is core to successful gameplay. See [[fleet-and-ships]] for ship details.

## Starbases

Each Starbase in the Galia Expanse functions as an independent economic hub. With the Dawn of the Freighter update (2025), each Starbase operates an insulated local marketplace where prices vary based on local supply and demand. This creates arbitrage opportunities and incentivizes trade routes between Starbases.

Starbases are where players refine resources, craft items, resupply fleets, and access the local marketplace.

## On-Chain Architecture

All SAGE gameplay is executed through Solana programs (smart contracts). Fleet states, resource balances, marketplace orders, and crafting recipes are recorded on-chain. This means the game economy is transparent, composable, and verifiable by anyone with access to the Solana blockchain.
