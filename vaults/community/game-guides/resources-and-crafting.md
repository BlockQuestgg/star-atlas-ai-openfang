# Resources & Crafting Guide

The Star Atlas economy is built on a resource extraction and crafting pipeline. Players mine raw materials, refine them into usable supplies, and craft advanced components.

## Raw Resources (R20)

Raw resources are extracted from asteroid fields and planetary bodies through mining operations in [[sage-overview|SAGE Labs]]. The four base raw resources are:

| Raw Resource | Refined Into | Primary Use |
|-------------|-------------|-------------|
| Hydrogen | Fuel | Fleet movement |
| Organics | Food | Crew sustenance |
| Iron | Toolkits | Ship maintenance and repair |
| Copper | Ammunition | Mining operations and combat |

These four are sometimes referred to as "R4 raws" because they refine into the four essential supplies. The broader R20 resource set includes additional materials used in advanced crafting recipes.

## Refined Resources (R4)

The four essential refined resources that every fleet needs:

- **Fuel** (from Hydrogen) — consumed during fleet movement between sectors and Starbases
- **Food** (from Organics) — consumed over time to sustain crew; fleets without food become stranded
- **Toolkits** (from Iron) — used for ship repairs and maintenance (note: not consumed in current SAGE Labs, reserved for future mechanics)
- **Ammunition** (from Copper) — consumed during mining operations; also used in combat

Refining happens at Starbases using crafting recipes.

## Crafting System

Crafting in SAGE Labs transforms raw and refined resources into higher-value items:

- **Basic crafting** — Refining raw resources into R4 supplies at Starbases
- **Component crafting** — Building ship components, upgrades, and advanced materials
- **Advanced recipes** — Radiation absorbers, frameworks, crystal lattice, and other specialty items

Crafting experience is earned through production, with advanced recipes expected to expand significantly with the [[roadmap-2026|C4 update]].

## Mining Mechanics

Mining requires a fleet with Ammunition and access to a resource-bearing sector:

1. Move your fleet to a sector with minable resources
2. Initiate mining (consumes Ammunition)
3. Extracted raw resources are added to fleet cargo
4. Return to a Starbase to refine or sell

Mining efficiency depends on fleet composition — larger ships and mining-specialized vessels extract resources faster.

## SDU Scanning

Survey Data Units (SDUs) are discovered through scanning operations:

- Fleets scan sectors to discover SDUs
- SDUs contain valuable data packages with resources and rare items
- Faction-based bonuses apply when scanning in your faction's territory
- SDU scanning is a distinct loop from mining, appealing to exploration-focused players

## Local Marketplaces

With the Dawn of the Freighter update, each Starbase has an independent local marketplace. Resource prices vary by location based on supply and demand, creating opportunities for trade routes and arbitrage between Starbases. See [[galactic-marketplace]] for more on trading.
