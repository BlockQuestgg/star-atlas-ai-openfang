# 2026 Roadmap — C4, Holosim, UE5 Season 0

2026 is shaping up as a transformative year for Star Atlas, with three major development tracks converging: the SAGE C4 overhaul, Holosim narrative chapters, and UE5 Season 0.

## SAGE C4 — The Complete Overhaul

C4 is a comprehensive overhaul of [[sage-overview|SAGE Labs]] that fundamentally changes how the game functions. It is not expected before late March, and more realistically April 2026.

### The Four C's

C4 introduces four interconnected gameplay systems:

- **Combat** — On-chain combat mechanics that raise the stakes for all SAGE activities. Combat is considered essential as it makes gameplay more exciting and consequential.
- **Claim Stakes** — Territorial control mechanics within SAGE
- **Cultivation Claims** — Resource cultivation and land management
- **Council Rank** — Reputation/rank system tied to governance
- **Crafting Habs** — Expanded crafting infrastructure

### What Changes

C4 brings a "Cambrian explosion" of resources, recipes, and ship components to craft. This expansion encourages player specialization and adds new dimensions to economic strategy. The resource and crafting system will be dramatically deeper than the current R4/R20 setup described in [[resources-and-crafting]].

### PTR Status (as of March 2026)

All four C's of SAGE C4 gameplay have been fully implemented and are live in internal testing. The team is in the bug-fixing and polishing phase. The public test release (PTR) is planned for the z.ink devnet once an internal z.ink upgrade completes and internal playtesting wraps up.

## Holosim — Narrative Combat Chapters

Holosim is the UE5-based combat and narrative module, delivering playable story episodes.

### Chapter 2: Mining & Territorial Control

Holosim Chapter 2 launched on March 10, 2026 after several delays. Key features:

- **Duration:** 5 weeks
- **Focus:** Mining and territorial control mechanics
- **Faction storylines:** All three [[factions|factions]] (MUD, ONI, Ustur) get their own storyline
- **NPC avatars:** Animated NPCs with spoken text present quests
- **New Combat UI:** Significantly enhanced combat systems and tracking
- **zProfile integration:** Player profile system integration
- **Prizes:** $10k worth of assets for participants

The team now has a functioning pipeline for producing new chapters more easily, and new chapters should roll out throughout 2026.

### Future Chapters

The development pipeline established with Chapter 2 means subsequent chapters can be produced faster. Each chapter introduces new mechanics and advances the faction narratives.

## UE5 Season 0 — The Full Experience

UE5 development continues to expand the visual Galia experience:

### What Is Season 0

Season 0 will be the first iteration of the UE5 game to deliver a **complete game experience** — serving as the baseline for future seasonal content. It puts players in the middle of a large-scale Tufa invasion of Ioki, with combat on space stations and on the ground.

### Galia Update

The UE5 team is working on adding progression and a storyline to improve the Galia game mode, addressing feedback that the visual experience needs more directed gameplay.

## SAGE-UE5 Convergence

The long-term vision connects SAGE and UE5:

- Territorial control in SAGE can trigger Conquest instances in UE5
- When outer defenses fall in SAGE, a Conquest instance opens in UE5 for the final fight
- This creates a bridge between the strategic economic layer (SAGE) and the visceral combat layer (UE5)
