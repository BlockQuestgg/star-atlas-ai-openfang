# Galactic Marketplace & NFT Economy

The Galactic Marketplace is the central trading hub of the Star Atlas metaverse, connecting players to buy and sell ships, resources, and other in-game assets.

## Marketplace Structure

The marketplace operates at two levels:

**Global Marketplace** — The primary marketplace at [play.staratlas.com/market](https://play.staratlas.com/market/) where players trade ship NFTs, cosmetics, and other global assets using [[token-economics|ATLAS tokens]].

**Local Starbase Marketplaces** — Introduced with the Dawn of the Freighter update (2025), each Starbase in the [[galia-expanse|Galia Expanse]] operates an independent local market. Prices for resources and crafted goods vary by location based on local supply and demand, creating trade route opportunities.

## What Can Be Traded

- **Ships** — NFT spaceships of all classes and rarities (see [[fleet-and-ships]])
- **Resources** — Raw materials (Hydrogen, Organics, Iron, Copper) and refined R4 supplies (see [[resources-and-crafting]])
- **Crafted Items** — Components, upgrades, and advanced materials
- **Cosmetics and Collectibles** — Visual items and limited-edition assets

## NFT Ships

Ship NFTs are the core tradeable assets in Star Atlas:

- Ships are minted as Solana NFTs (using compressed NFT standards)
- Rarity tiers: Common, Uncommon, Rare, Epic, Legendary, Anomaly
- Ships can be staked into [[sage-overview|SAGE Labs]] or held for trading
- Galactic Asset Offerings (GAOs) are periodic primary sales of new ship NFTs
- Secondary trading occurs both on the Galactic Marketplace and external Solana NFT marketplaces (Tensor, Magic Eden)

## Local Market Economics

The local marketplace system creates emergent economic gameplay:

- Each Starbase has independent pricing based on local supply/demand
- Players can profit by hauling goods between Starbases (using [[fleet-and-ships|Freighter-class ships]])
- Resource scarcity at remote Starbases drives higher prices
- Faction territory influences resource availability and pricing

## Marketplace Fees

Trading on the marketplace incurs fees that serve as economic sinks in the [[token-economics|ATLAS economy]]. Fee parameters can be adjusted through [[dao-overview|DAO governance]].
