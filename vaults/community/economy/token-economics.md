# Token Economics — ATLAS & POLIS

Star Atlas operates a dual-token economy on the Solana blockchain. Each token serves a distinct purpose: ATLAS powers the in-game economy, while POLIS governs the metaverse's political layer.

## ATLAS — The In-Game Currency

ATLAS is the primary medium of exchange within the Star Atlas metaverse.

**Supply:** 36,000,000,000 (36 billion) total supply. ATLAS has inflationary tokenomics — new tokens are minted as player rewards to sustain the growing in-game economy.

**Utility:**
- Purchasing ships, resources, and items on the [[galactic-marketplace|Galactic Marketplace]]
- In-game transactions (refueling, repairing, crafting)
- Rewarding players for economic activity in [[sage-overview|SAGE Labs]]
- Marketplace fees and economic sinks

**Economic Design:** ATLAS is designed to circulate — it flows from players to the ecosystem through purchases and fees, and back to players through gameplay rewards. The DAO can adjust economic dials like reward distribution rates, sink mechanisms, and exchange fees to maintain economic health.

## POLIS — The Governance Token

POLIS represents political power in the Star Atlas metaverse. See [[dao-overview]] and [[pip-lifecycle]] for full governance details.

**Supply:** 360,000,000 (360 million) fixed supply. Unlike ATLAS, POLIS is not inflationary — it is designed as a long-term store of value.

**Utility:**
- Voting on [[pip-lifecycle|Polity Improvement Proposals (PIPs)]]
- Governing treasury allocations
- Setting game parameters (NFT marketplace fees, economic dials)
- Accruing POLIS rewards through staking/locking
- Expressing political rights within the metaverse

**Locking Mechanics:** To exercise voting power, POLIS must be locked in the POLIS Locker protocol. Voting power is proportional to both the amount locked and the remaining lock duration. For example, 1,000 POLIS locked for 4 years yields 4,000 voting power. This time-weighted mechanism incentivizes long-term commitment and mitigates flash-loan governance attacks.

## Dual-Token Interaction

The two tokens serve complementary roles:

- **ATLAS** is the economic engine — inflationary, high-velocity, used for daily transactions
- **POLIS** is the political engine — fixed supply, locked for governance, accrues value from ecosystem growth

The Star Atlas DAO (governed by POLIS holders) has authority over economic parameters that affect ATLAS circulation, creating a feedback loop between governance decisions and economic outcomes.

## Economic Sinks and Sources

**Sources** (tokens entering circulation): gameplay rewards, mining yields, SDU scanning rewards, staking emissions.

**Sinks** (tokens leaving circulation): marketplace fees, crafting costs, ship maintenance, fuel and supply consumption, POLIS locking.

The balance between sources and sinks determines the health of the in-game economy. The DAO can adjust these parameters through governance votes.
