# Domain Expertise — Creative Strategy

## Star Atlas Ecosystem Context

Ideas must be grounded in the Star Atlas reality:

- **Star Atlas** — grand strategy space game on Solana, combining on-chain
  economics with AAA-quality visuals
- **Community** — global, crypto-native, passionate about space/sci-fi
- **DAO governance** — POLIS holders vote on proposals (PIPs)
- **Multi-platform** — SAGE (browser), Holosim (mobile), UE5 (desktop)
- **Economy** — ATLAS utility token, ship/structure NFTs, resource markets

## Creative Frameworks

When generating ideas, try these lenses:

### Inversion
What's the opposite of what every Web3 game does? If all crypto games
focus on earning, what about pure fun? If all DAOs are serious, what
about playful governance?

### Adjacent Possible
What's one step beyond current plans? If SAGE fleet battles work, what's
the next evolution? Esports league? Cross-game competitions?

### Transplant
What works in another industry that nobody has applied to Web3 gaming?
How do esports orgs work? Film festivals? Space programs? Music tours?

### Constraint as Feature
What limitation could become a selling point? Blockchain is slow? That
means decisions are deliberate. Economy is volatile? That means dynamic
gameplay. Community is small? That means tight-knit and influential.

### Who's Not in the Room?
Who doesn't play Star Atlas but should? Traditional gamers? Sci-fi fans?
Space enthusiasts? What would bring them in?

## Brainstorm Quality Signals

Good brainstorm output:
- Specific enough to act on (not "do marketing" but "partner with a
  sci-fi book club for a lore-themed reading + gaming event")
- Connected to what Star Atlas already has or is building
- Acknowledges obstacles honestly
- Suggests a concrete first step
- Would make a community member say "huh, I hadn't thought of that"

Weak brainstorm output:
- Generic advice that applies to any Web3 project
- Repeating what's already being done
- Ideas requiring resources the community doesn't have
- Pure fantasy disconnected from the ecosystem's stage
