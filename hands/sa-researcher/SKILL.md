# Domain Expertise — Web3 Gaming Research

## Star Atlas Context

Star Atlas is a grand strategy game of space exploration, territorial
conquest, and political domination built on the Solana blockchain.

### Key Components

- **SAGE (Star Atlas Golden Era)** — browser-based strategy game, on-chain
- **Holosim** — mobile game companion
- **UE5 Showroom** — Unreal Engine 5 visual experience
- **ATLAS** — utility token (in-game currency)
- **POLIS** — governance token (DAO voting power via vote-escrow)

### Ecosystem

- **Solana blockchain** — high-throughput, low-cost L1
- **Star Atlas DAO** — governance via POLIS voting-escrow
- **F-KIT** — developer toolkit for building on Star Atlas
- **Marketplace** — NFT trading for ships, structures, resources

## Solana Ecosystem Knowledge

### Key Infrastructure

- SPL Token program, Token-2022 extensions
- Metaplex NFT standard
- Serum/OpenBook DEX
- Marinade, Jupiter, Raydium (DeFi protocols)

### Market Data Sources

## Research Brief Conventions

Follow obsidian-markdown conventions:
- YAML frontmatter: title, date, tags, source, status
- Kebab-case filenames with date prefix: `2026-03-12-solana-defi-trends.md`
- Wikilinks for internal references: `[[related-note]]`
- Callouts for important context: `> [!info] Title`

## Source Evaluation (CRAAP Framework)

Evaluate sources using:
- **Currency:** When was it published/updated?
- **Relevance:** Does it address the specific research question?
- **Authority:** Who published it? What are their credentials?
- **Accuracy:** Is it supported by evidence? Can claims be verified?
- **Purpose:** Why does this information exist? Any bias?

Rate each source: High / Medium / Low credibility.

## Crypto-Specific Evaluation

Additional checks for crypto/Web3 sources:
- **Conflict of interest:** Does the author hold tokens they're writing about?
- **Shill vs. analysis:** Is this promotion or genuine analysis?
- **On-chain verification:** Can claims be verified on-chain?
- **Date sensitivity:** Crypto markets move fast — is this still relevant?
