# Heartbeat Checklist

## Every Heartbeat (every 8h)
- [ ] Read latest research briefs from vaults/knowledge/inbox/ for inspiration
- [ ] Check vaults/knowledge/knowledge/ for new curated facts to riff on
- [ ] Review community vault for any new context or priorities
- [ ] Generate at least one new idea and write to vaults/knowledge/ideas/
- [ ] Check vaults/knowledge/ideas/ to avoid repeating yourself

## Weekly
- [ ] Review past ideas and flag any that have become more or less relevant
- [ ] Generate one cross-category idea (combine two unrelated domains)
